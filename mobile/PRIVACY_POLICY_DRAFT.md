# Privacy Policy for HomeSarthi

**Last Updated: February 21, 2026**

HomeSarthi ("we", "our", or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your personal information is collected, used, and disclosed by HomeSarthi.

This Privacy Policy applies to our mobile application "HomeSarthi" and our related services. By accessing or using our Service, you signify that you have read, understood, and agree to our collection, storage, use, and disclosure of your personal information as described in this Privacy Policy.

## 1. Information We Collect

### A. Information You Provide to Us
*   **Account Information:** When you register, we collect your name, email address, phone number, and password.
*   **Profile Information:** You may provide a profile picture and other details.
*   **Property Details (Owners):** If you are an owner, we collect details about your property, including address, photos, and rent information.
*   **Communications:** Records of chats and support requests between users and with our team.

### B. Information Collected Automatically
*   **Location Data:** With your permission, we collect precise or approximate location data from your mobile device to show nearby rooms and provide mapping services.
*   **Device Information:** We collect information about the device you use (model, OS version, unique device identifiers).
*   **Usage Data:** Information about how you interact with our app (searches, rooms viewed, clicks).

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:
*   **To Provide Services:** To facilitate room searches, bookings, and connections between students and owners.
*   **To Improve Our App:** To analyze usage patterns and fix bugs.
*   **Communication:** To send you notifications about messages, booking updates, and security alerts.
*   **Safety and Security:** To verify listings and protect against fraudulent activity.

## 3. Sharing of Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. We share data only in the following cases:
*   **Between Users:** Students see owner contact info and property details; Owners see student names/profiles for inquiries.
*   **Service Providers:** We use third-party tools like Google Maps (for location), Cloudinary (for image storage), and Expo (for notifications).
*   **Legal Requirements:** If required by law or to protect our rights.

## 4. Data Security

We use industry-standard encryption (SSL/TLS) and secure database practices to protect your data. However, no method of transmission over the internet is 100% secure.

## 5. Your Rights and Choices

*   **Access and Update:** You can update your profile info inside the app.
*   **Location Permissions:** You can enable or disable location access via device settings.
*   **Account Deletion:** You may request to delete your account by contacting us at homesarthi247@gmail.com.

## 6. Children's Privacy

Our services are intended for students (typically 18+) and property owners. We do not knowingly collect data from children under 13.

## 7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
